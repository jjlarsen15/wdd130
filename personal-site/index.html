The focus of the site is to provide organized, easy-to-read information about significant characters, locations, and plots of each Star Wars movie. The primary audience includes both longtime fans looking to refresh their memory and newcomers to the franchise who want a quick and reliable way to get up to speed. This site will serve as a central guide for anyone curious about the movies, needing context for a character, or simply wanting to explore the galaxy far, far away. Another feature of the website will be short, spoiler-aware synopses of each film.
