General Grievous

Species: Kaleesh (cyborg)

Homeworld: Kalee

Affiliation: Confederacy of Independent Systems (Separatists)

Lightsaber Colors: Various (taken from Jedi victims)

Occupation: Supreme Commander of the Droid Army

Notable Feature: Cybernetic body with multiple arms

Character Synopsis

General Grievous was a fearsome cyborg warlord and the Supreme Commander of the Separatist droid army during the Clone Wars. Originally a Kaleesh warrior named Qymaen jai Sheelal, he was transformed into a cyborg by the Sith Lord Count Dooku. Known for his brutal combat tactics and collection of lightsabers from slain Jedi, Grievous became a symbol of terror across the galaxy. Despite his mechanical body, he retained a hatred for the Jedi and immense tactical skill. He met his end at the hands of Obi-Wan Kenobi on the planet Utapau in Revenge of the Sith.
