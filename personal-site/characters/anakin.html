Anakin Skywalker

Species: Human

Homeworld: Tatooine

Affiliation: Jedi Order (formerly), Galactic Republic, Galactic Empire

Lightsaber Color: Blue (as Jedi), Red (as Sith)

Also Known As: Darth Vader

Master: Obi-Wan Kenobi

Character Synopsis

Anakin Skywalker was a Jedi Knight and the prophesied Chosen One, believed to bring balance to the Force. Born into slavery on Tatooine, his exceptional Force sensitivity was discovered by Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn. Anakin was trained by Obi-Wan Kenobi and became a heroic figure in the Clone Wars. However, his fear of loss and desire for power led him down a dark path, ultimately becoming the Sith Lord Darth Vader. As Vader, he served the Emperor and hunted the remaining Jedi. In the end, Anakin found redemption through the love of his son, Luke Skywalker, and fulfilled the prophecy by destroying Emperor Palpatine.
