Qui-Gon Jinn

Species: Human

Homeworld: Coruscant

Affiliation: Jedi Order

Lightsaber Color: Green

Master: Dooku (formerly)

Apprentice: Obi-Wan Kenobi

Character Synopsis

Qui-Gon Jinn was a Jedi Master known for his unorthodox methods and strong belief in the Living Force. He was a wise and skilled warrior, but often disagreed with the Jedi Council's strict adherence to tradition. Qui-Gon’s most significant act in the *Phantom Menace* was discovering Anakin Skywalker on Tatooine, believing him to be the Chosen One prophesied to bring balance to the Force. Despite the Jedi Council’s skepticism, Qui-Gon was determined to train Anakin. He was also a mentor to Obi-Wan Kenobi, and their partnership was central to the events of *The Phantom Menace*. Qui-Gon’s dedication to the Force and his sense of destiny ultimately led him to a tragic confrontation with Darth Maul, where he was mortally wounded. His legacy, however, lived on through his teachings and his role in shaping Anakin’s future.
