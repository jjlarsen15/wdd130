Darth Maul

Species: Dathomirian Zabrak

Homeworld: Dathomir

Affiliation: Sith, Darth Sidious

Lightsaber Color: Red (dual-bladed)

Also Known As: The Sith Lord, Maul

Master: Darth Sidious

Apprentice: None

Character Synopsis

Darth Maul was a fearsome Sith Lord trained by Darth Sidious, and a key figure in *The Phantom Menace*. Known for his menacing appearance with a red and black face, as well as his double-bladed lightsaber, Maul was sent to hunt down Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi after they were dispatched to protect Queen Amidala. His fierce combat skills and mastery of the dark side made him a formidable adversary, culminating in a duel with Obi-Wan and Qui-Gon on Naboo. Although Maul was ultimately defeated by Obi-Wan, his legacy as a terrifying figure in the Star Wars universe lives on.
