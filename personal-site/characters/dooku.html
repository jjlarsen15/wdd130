Count Dooku

Species: Human

Homeworld: Serenno

Affiliation: Sith, Confederacy of Independent Systems

Lightsaber Color: Red

Also Known As: Darth Tyranus

Former Master: Yoda

Apprentice: General Grievous (trained in lightsaber combat)

Character Synopsis

Count Dooku, also known as Darth Tyranus, was once a respected Jedi Master and the padawan of Yoda himself. Disillusioned with the Jedi Order and the corruption within the Republic, he left the Jedi and eventually became a Sith Lord under the mentorship of Darth Sidious. As the political leader of the Separatists, Dooku masterminded the Clone Wars, manipulating both sides of the conflict to serve his master's goals. Elegant and charismatic, Dooku was a formidable duelist and a cunning strategist. His ambition and loyalty to the Sith ultimately led to his downfall at the hands of Anakin Skywalker.
