Obi-Wan Kenobi

Species: Human

Homeworld: Stewjon

Lightsaber Color: Blue

Occupation: Jedi Master, General

Character Synopsis

Obi-Wan Kenobi was a wise and powerful Jedi Master who played a central role in the Clone Wars and the rise and fall of Anakin Skywalker. Trained by Qui-Gon Jinn and later serving as a mentor to Anakin, Obi-Wan was known for his calm demeanor, loyalty, and strong connection to the Force. He fought bravely in countless battles, including his iconic duel with Anakin on Mustafar. After going into hiding, Obi-Wan watched over Luke Skywalker on Tatooine until their fateful meeting, guiding Luke to begin his own Jedi journey. He ultimately became one with the Force after sacrificing himself aboard the Death Star.
