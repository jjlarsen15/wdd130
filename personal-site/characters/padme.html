Padmé Amidala

Species: Human

Homeworld: Naboo

Affiliation: Galactic Republic

Role: Queen of Naboo, Senator

Also Known As: Padmé Naberrie

Spouse: Anakin Skywalker

Children: Luke Skywalker, Leia Organa

Character Synopsis

Padmé Amidala, born Padmé Naberrie, was the Queen of Naboo during the Trade Federation invasion and later served as a senator in the Galactic Senate. As a young queen, she fought against the invasion of her homeworld and negotiated the end of the conflict. Following her time as queen, she became a strong advocate for peace and diplomacy as a senator, ultimately being instrumental in the creation of the Galactic Republic's legislative framework during the Clone Wars. Throughout her life, Padmé was a passionate leader, a courageous warrior, and a devoted mother. She was also the secret wife of Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker and the mother of the twins Luke and Leia.
