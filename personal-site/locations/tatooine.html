Tatooine

Sector: Outer Rim Territories

Climate: Desert, arid, and harsh

Native Species: Jawas, Tusken Raiders (Sand People), Hutts

Notable Locations: Mos Eisley, Lars Homestead, Mos Espa

Political Affiliation: Independent, controlled by local crime lords

Location Synopsis

Tatooine is a harsh, barren desert planet located in the Outer Rim Territories. Despite its inhospitable environment, it plays a pivotal role in the Star Wars saga. It is where Anakin Skywalker was discovered as a child and where Luke Skywalker later grows up. The planet is home to nomadic bands of Tusken Raiders, as well as scavenging Jawas. Major settlements include Mos Eisley, a notorious hive of scum and villainy, and Mos Espa, where Anakin Skywalker participated in the Boonta Eve podrace. Tatooine's political situation is unstable, with local crime lords such as Jabba the Hutt exerting control over much of the planet. It’s a place of hidden secrets and an essential starting point for the characters' journeys.
