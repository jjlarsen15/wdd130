Kamino

Planet Type: Ocean Planet

Location: Outer Rim Territories, Unknown Regions

Key Features: Constant stormy weather, vast oceans, cloning facilities

Inhabitants: Kaminoans, Clones

Notable Events: Creation of the Clone Army

Location Synopsis

Kamino is a mysterious, storm-lashed ocean planet located in the far reaches of the galaxy's Unknown Regions. Known for its continuous rainstorms and vast oceans, Kamino is home to the Kaminoans, a species of tall, graceful beings known for their expertise in cloning. It was on Kamino that the Galactic Republic’s Clone Army was secretly created, designed to fight in the Clone Wars. The planet’s isolated location and hidden nature made it the perfect place for the secretive creation of the clones, which were engineered from the genetic material of bounty hunter Jango Fett. The planet played a critical role in the events of the prequel trilogy, particularly in "Attack of the Clones."
