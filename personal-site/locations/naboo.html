Naboo

Sector: Mid Rim

Climate: Temperate, lush, and green with forests, lakes, and plains

Native Species: Humans, Gungans

Notable Locations: Theed, Otoh Gunga, Lake Country

Political Affiliation: Galactic Republic, independent world

Location Synopsis

Naboo is a tranquil, beautiful planet known for its lush landscapes and harmonious balance between nature and civilization. It is the homeworld of Padmé Amidala, and it plays a significant role in *The Phantom Menace* as the backdrop for the conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Trade Federation. The planet is notable for its dual society: the peaceful and aristocratic human population in the capital city of Theed, and the aquatic Gungan species who live in the vast swamps of Otoh Gunga. Naboo's people are known for their diplomatic efforts and love of peace, and the planet’s royalty and senate are key players in the galaxy’s political landscape.
