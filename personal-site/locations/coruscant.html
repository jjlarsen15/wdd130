Coruscant

Planet Type: Ecumenopolis (City-Covered Planet)

Location: Core Worlds

Key Features: Galactic capital, towering skyscrapers, Jedi Temple

Inhabitants: Humans, Senators, Jedi, Droids

Notable Events: Galactic Senate, Jedi Order, Battle of Coruscant

Location Synopsis

Coruscant is the bustling capital of the galaxy and the heart of both the Galactic Republic and later the Galactic Empire. This ecumenopolis, meaning a planet entirely covered by a sprawling cityscape, is home to towering skyscrapers, massive starports, and important institutions, including the Galactic Senate and the Jedi Temple. The planet's central location in the Core Worlds made it the political, cultural, and economic center of the galaxy. Coruscant was a focal point in the rise and fall of the Republic, witnessing significant events such as the Battle of Coruscant and the eventual transformation of the Republic into the Empire. The Jedi Order’s headquarters, the Jedi Temple, stood on Coruscant for millennia until it was destroyed during the rise of the Empire. The planet continues to be a symbol of galactic power and intrigue.
