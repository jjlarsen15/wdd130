Alderaan

Sector: Core Worlds

Climate: Temperate, lush, and scenic

Native Species: Humans

Notable Locations: Aldera, Capital city

Political Affiliation: Galactic Republic

Location Synopsis

Alderaan is a peaceful and beautiful planet in the Core Worlds, known for its stunning landscapes, lush forests, and temperate climate. It is the homeworld of Princess Leia Organa and one of the most influential members of the Galactic Republic. Its people value diplomacy and peaceful resolution to conflicts over violence and war. Alderaan is also known for its rich culture and technology, but it would ultimately become a tragic symbol of the Empire's cruelty. The planet was destroyed by the first Death Star in *A New Hope* as a demonstration of its destructive power. Despite its brief appearance in *The Phantom Menace*, Alderaan's legacy is deeply embedded in the Star Wars saga, representing hope, peace, and sacrifice.
