Geonosis

Planet Type: Rocky Desert Planet

Location: Outer Rim Territories

Key Features: Vast deserts, canyon-like terrain, Geonosian hives

Inhabitants: Geonosians, Battle Droids

Notable Events: Battle of Geonosis, Creation of the Droid Army

Location Synopsis

Geonosis is a harsh, desert planet located in the Outer Rim Territories. Its landscape is characterized by rocky deserts, deep canyons, and unique structures, including the massive Geonosian hives. The planet is best known for being the site of the first major battle of the Clone Wars, the Battle of Geonosis, where the Republic’s clone army clashed with the Separatists’ droid forces. Geonosis also played a pivotal role in the creation of the Droid Army, as the planet's inhabitants, the Geonosians, were responsible for manufacturing the battle droids under the direction of Count Dooku and the Sith. The planet’s vast, isolated desert terrain hides many secrets, including the plans for the construction of the Death Star, which were discovered in the subsequent events of the Star Wars saga.
