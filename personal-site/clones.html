Attack of the Clones

Movie Synopsis

Set ten years after *The Phantom Menace*, *Attack of the Clones* follows Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi as they uncover a secret plot to create an army for the Republic. Amid political unrest and the rise of the Separatists, Anakin begins to form a connection with Padmé Amidala. The film culminates in the outbreak of the Clone Wars, a galaxy-wide conflict that will change the fate of the galaxy. Meanwhile, Jedi Knight Obi-Wan uncovers a connection to the mysterious Count Dooku and the galaxy's growing instability.
