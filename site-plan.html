Content

Home Page

Website Overview
My website will be a resource hub dedicated to the Star Wars universe. The focus of the site will be to provide organized, easy-to-read information about the characters, locations, and plots of each Star Wars movie.

Simple Summaries
Another feature of the website will be short, spoiler-aware synopses of each film. The goal is to give readers a clear sense of what happens in each movie without overwhelming them with too much detail.

Location Highlights
In addition to characters and synopses, I will also include a section for notable locations introduced in each movie. Each location page will give a brief history and explain its relevance to the larger saga.

Audience Purpose
Visitors can expect the site to answer questions such as: “Who is that character?” “What happens in this movie?” “Where is this scene set?” The site provides these answers clearly and efficiently.

User Activities
Visitors might want to use the site while watching the movies, during fan fiction writing, or while prepping for a rewatch marathon. It’s also helpful to parents introducing Star Wars to kids.

Site Advantage
What makes my site different from other Star Wars websites is the clean organization and movie-by-movie breakdown. It’s user-friendly and ideal for quick reference.

Characters & Locations

Character Descriptions
The site will have brief bios for each major character, including their role, affiliations, and key moments. These will help users remember who is who and where they appeared.

Planet Introductions
Each major location will include a brief intro, its importance in the story, and key events that happened there.

Movie Synopses

Movie Summaries
The site will feature spoiler-aware summaries of each film, covering the main conflicts, settings, and character arcs.

Canon-Only Content
The summaries and information will stay within movie canon, making it easier for fans to find trustworthy, relevant information.
